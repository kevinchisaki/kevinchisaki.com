My Role

Internship / Identity / UX / Web / Motion / Print

Previously, I worked at Riverbed Technology as a Multimedia Designer on their Marketing Communications design team for 2.5 years. In this position, I produced event and tradeshow creative assets including collateral material, corporate branded signage, and infographics to strengthen company brand.

I also took part in leading the design process of in-house websites for our sales kick off events for two years. Over the course of my time at Riverbed I worked with senior designers, project managers, web developers, and content writers during the process of each project before deliverables were published.

Riverbed Partner Portal

During my second year at Riverbed I was in charge of updating the new icons for the Riverbed Partner Portal. The Riverbed Partner Portal is a control center for users to buy, deploy, and extend Riverbed solutions. During the design process I worked closely with developers and customers to create memorable and relative icons for each Riverbed product. These icons were used for the desktop version of the portal. Before the mobile version was created, I decided to design my own version of the partner portal as a side project.

Riverbed Sales Kick Off Mobile Agenda

Every year, Riverbed has a Sales Kick Off (SKO) event to start off their new year of products and updates. Hundreds of people attend the event and come from all over the world to see the keynotes, seminars, and new partner products. As the main web developer and designer on the Riverbed Marketing Design team I was in charge of leading the design process and development of the mobile agenda for SKO.

The mobile agenda was used to help attendees navigate around the venue and plan out their whole schedule throughout the event. The agenda is fully responsive and can be used on mobile or desktop. More than 300+ people use this mobile agenda throughout SKO week and it has always been a proud project for me while working for Riverbed.
